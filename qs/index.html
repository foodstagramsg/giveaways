About Lawry's

Celebrating Special Occasions since 1938

The 6500 square feet restaurant with a seating capacity of up to 170 is located on the fourth floor of the brand new Mandarin Hotel Gallery.

Expect panoramic views of the bustling Orchard Road with our stunning 4meter height floor to ceiling dows whilst enjoying your meal.

With hues of Brown, Gold and Champagne colour as the theme colours, the restaurant exudes a charming and majestic setting yet one will feel cosy and unperturbed while dining in.
